Mysterians

by Charles Iliya Krempeaux

Mysterians was a toy-line from 1983 developed by people at or working with Knickerbocker Toys. The Mysterians toy-line was canceled before making it to the market.

Some of the Mysterians toys were re-named, sometimes re-colored, and released as part of the Transformers toy-line. Which would make Mysterians a pre-Transformers.

Mysterians Sub-Lines

The Mysterians toy-line had 2 sub-lines:

  • The Mobile Force, and
  • The Hidden Force.

Mysterians: The Mobile Force

Out of the two Mysterians sub-lines, the Mobile Force sub-line is the sub-line of Mysterians that will probably seem more familiar, because they were re-named, sometimes re-colored, and released as part of the Transformers toy-line.

The toys that were part of the Mobile Force sub-line:

  • Blazar,
  • Jeepar
  • Speedar
  • Truckar

These were initially re-named, sometimes re-colored, and released as transformers as:

  • Mysterians Blazar became Transformers Gears;
  • Mysterians Jeepar became Transformers Brawn;
  • Mysterians Speedar became Transformers Windcharger;
  • Mysterians Truckar became Transformers Huffer.

If one also includes re-colors of all these toys, then the list would include:

  • Mysterians Blazar became Transformers Gears, Swerve, Small Foot;
  • Mysterians Jeepar became Transformers Brawn, Outback, Jibe;
  • Mysterians Speedar became Transformers Windcharger, Tailgate, Camaro.
  • Mysterians Truckar became Transformers Huffer, Pipes, Road Ranger.
